Leadership & Management

Mr. Mangesh Gawai

Director & Core Leadership

With 35 years of experience in the technology field, Mangesh Gawai founded New Special Island as Director in Oman, bringing a passion for exploring new technology and introducing it to society.

As the first entrepreneur to introduce smart film in Oman, he has quickly expanded his smart home and office automation business to the Middle East and India, ensuring that every product meets NSI's high standards for quality and reliability.

Featured Leader: Recognized for his significant contributions to the regional smart technology sector, Mr. Gawai's work and the rapid growth of NSI have been selected for an exclusive cover-story feature in Asia Business Outlook Magazine.
