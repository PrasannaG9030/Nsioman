The 4 Main Types of Cameras

NSI Oman installs the primary form-factors of surveillance cameras designed to fit different architectural needs and viewing angles.

Bullet Cameras

Highly visible, cylindrical cameras mounted to point towards a specific area. Excellent for long-distance viewing and outdoor deterrence.

Variations & Sub-types:

  • Full-Color Bullet: Maintains color imaging 24/7.
  • Active Deterrence: Features built-in sirens and strobe lights.
  • Motorized Varifocal: Allows adjusting zoom and focus remotely.
Get Quote

Dome Cameras

Discreet, dome-shaped cameras typically housed in impact-resistant glass. Best for indoor ceilings or covered outdoor spaces.

Variations & Sub-types:

  • Vandal-Proof Dome (IK10): Highly resistant to physical impacts.
  • IR Dome: Integrated infrared LEDs for pitch-black night vision.
  • Mini Dome: Ultra-compact design for maximum discretion.
Get Quote

Turret / Eyeball Cameras

Ball-and-socket design providing easy angle adjustment without a glass dome, reducing IR glare and cobweb interference.

Variations & Sub-types:

  • Audio-Enabled Turret: Built-in microphone for high-quality audio recording.
  • Dual-Illumination: Switches from IR to white light when motion is detected.
  • Wide-Angle Eyeball: Maximizes the coverage area for open spaces.
Get Quote

PTZ Cameras

Pan, Tilt, and Zoom functionality. Actively adjust the camera lens via app to look around or zoom in up to 30x on specific details.

Variations & Sub-types:

  • Mini PTZ: Compact version suitable for residential and small retail use.
  • Long-Range Auto-Tracking: Automatically locks onto and follows moving objects.
  • Laser PTZ: Extreme long-distance night vision up to hundreds of meters.
