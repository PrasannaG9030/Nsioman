Smart Glass Film Solutions

Different Visions, Different Views. Transform any ordinary glass into a switchable privacy barrier at the touch of a button.

See Applications Request Setup

Instant privacy. Elegant design.

Smart Glass Film (PDLC) is a revolutionary technology applied directly to existing glass. When power is applied, the liquid crystals align and the glass becomes transparent. Turn the power off, and it instantly frosts over for complete privacy.

Total Privacy

Instantly block views into meeting rooms, bathrooms, or exterior windows while maintaining an elegant, minimalist aesthetic without bulky curtains.

UV & Heat Reduction

Even in its transparent state, smart film blocks up to 98% of harmful UV rays and significantly reduces solar heat gain, protecting your interiors.

Smart Control

Control opacity via a simple wall switch, dedicated remote control, or integrate it directly into your centralized Smart Home app.
